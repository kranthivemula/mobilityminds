About Us

Mobile as a CRM Channel

Overview

Service providers (Retailers, Brands, Sports Organizations, and Entertainment Networks etc) are always striving to enhance the understanding of their consumer needs, so that they can serve them better. Service providers face different challenges practically as well as technically to reach out to their consumers, understand their exact needs, deliver the contents real-time and ensure consumer involvement and satisfaction. The continuous increase in the mobile phone penetration in consumers has provided an excellent opportunity to service providers, to gain access and amplify their consumer reach and interaction.

Why?

Our solutions are explicitly based on principle Attain-Maintain-Retain. Our CRM solutions are exclusively targeted for consumer oriented businesses, addressing their precise business needs to improve and expand the Consumer Reach, Consumer Interaction, Feedback and Support. It offers a perfect solution for service provider’s requirements, combining consumer centric business logic and wireless/mobile technology services. Our solutions are developed with exclusive focus on the two key aspects – the consumer and the ability of service provider to deliver with consumer interest. These two key aspects form the core of all the consumer centric businesses. Our solutions enable service providers to gain consumer loyalty, retention and revenue.Using our CRM solutions service providers can reach out to their existing as well as new consumers, collect information on consumer interests, understand their exact needs,deliver the relevant contents, get feedback from consumers and increase interaction with them.

  • Solutions

    • Our solutions help service providers ensure consumer involvement, entertainment, interaction and satisfaction which will enable them to attain, maintain and retain their consumers.

  • Create device-optimized micro sites and mobile applications

  • Relevant content extraction and delivery to consumers based on type of consumer access
  • Manage and distribute mobile binary content including Images,Information,Video,Digital Cards,Themes,Merchandise,Bar Codes,Mobile Applications and other digital content
