About Us

Mobile as a CRM Channel

Mobile Promotions

Mobile Promotions helps service providers to create coupons and offers instantly and send it to a target group of consumers. CRM supports both text based and 1D/2D barcode based offers. The offers can be tied to the Mobile Loyalty Solution to target offers and promotions to the loyalty members.

Benefits

  • Increased opportunities to cross-sell and up-sell
  • Reinforces brand equity by making the differentiating benefits available at the hands of the consumers
  • Time effectiveness
  • Through integration of POS data can provide invaluable insights into consumer behaviors and provides unparalleled closed-loop analytics data

    • Mobile Content Management

    Mobile Content Management interfaces with the existing CMS/CRM systems of the service providers to extract relevant content and deliver it to the consumer’s mobile, based on the type of access by the consumer. Mobile Content Management allows service providers to manage and distribute mobile binary content including Images, Information, Video, Digital Cards, Themes, Merchandise, Bar Codes, Mobile Applications and other digital content.

    Benefits

  • Increased customer satisfaction
  • Reduction in call-center costs
  • Increased opportunities to cross-sell and up-sell
  • Reduction in printing/publishing costs
